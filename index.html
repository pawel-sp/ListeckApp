Listeck Privacy Policy


The Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what data the application collects, why it is needed and how you can change the way of collecting the data.

What kind of the information does the application collect?

The application collects following types of information:

  • User information (unique identifier and a full name)
  • Shopping lists
  • Shopping items assigned to shopping lists
  • Catalog with predefined products
  • Custom predefined products defined by the user

How does the application collect the data?

Listeck uses MongoDB to store user's data in cloud. That integration allows for keeping the same data for all at your devices and provide a full synchronization between them. It also ensures that you do not loose your data after deleting the application. For any further information please visit MongoDB Privacy Policy.

Why does the application collect the data?

Collecting the data is necessary to allow you to save all of the information which you can create, modify or delete inside the app. Without it, you would have to create them from scratch every time when you launch the app. Thanks to MongoDB integration, you and only you will have an access to your data at all of your devices authorized with your Apple ID.

Sign In with Apple

The authorization is required in order to assign your data to your own account. The application uses "Sign In with Apple" functionality to create a new account or fetch the already existing one. The only information which is obligatory to set is your full name, which is used to distinguish list's participants from each other whenever you assign shopping items to them. You can logout or delete your account by pressing your avatar on the main page. Deleting the account will also remove all of your shopping lists which you created and remove your data from any list which you are a participant of. Restoring deleted account is not supported but you can create a new account using the same Apple ID. The Settings of your iPhone also provides you a way to remove this app from the list of apps which your Apple ID uses with "Sign In with Apple". In order to do that, you need to open the Settings â” iCloud Account â” Password & Security â” Apps using Apple ID.

In App Purchases and the data

The application provides premium features. All of them you can buy via "In App Purchase" service provided by Apple. The application doesn't store any information about payment methods or any of the transactions. All of it is handled by Apple services and the application doesn't have an access to any of these types of data.

Sharing data with the other users

One of the premium features, which the application offers to buy is "Lists Sharing". You can send the invitation to any of your shopping lists to your friends or family in order to share the same shopping list. The shared lists are synced in real time between the devices and all the users who participate in. Every invitation expires after 24 hours if no one accepts it. The invitation link can be used only once and only by one person. If you want to invite more people to your shopping list, you need to send a new link to every person. Resigning from the list's participation is possible by opening a shopping list and selecting "Leave list" button.

Analytics

To improve the quality of the app, Listeck uses NewRelic to collect common data like crash logs, app versions, model of your device, etc. This tool allows to monitor and improve how the application works for the users. The application does not send any sensitive data or any data related to your iCloud account. For any further information please visit New Relic Services Notices.
